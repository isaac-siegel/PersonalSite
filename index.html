Bronco Scheduler

Born at a hackathon. Thousands of users, still going strong. (Don't worry, it isn't illegal. "Hack", as in "to create".)

I had an idea. We were all college students with a problem that took too long to solve. And we had to do it every time class registration came along! There was only one solution, make the computer do it.

Let's say, for your next quarter/semester at school, you knew that you wanted to take a specific Math class, Physics Class, and English Class. The only issue is, they are all offered at 10 different times, each!

That is a grand total of 1000 possible combinations of classes. Then you have to figure out what classes conflict with what and pretty soon you are trying to draw a giant chart on a piece of paper that will never fit it. Our site takes care of it. It'll figure out what works. And if you don't want to wake up before 2pm, that's fine. If you work a job Tuesday and Thursday, no problem. Filters galore.
